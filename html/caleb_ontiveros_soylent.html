Description

Soylent is an open source meal replacement, advertised as a "staple meal", available in liquid and powdered forms as a beverage, and as a solid-form meal bar. Wiki

Ingredients

  • Soy Protein Isolate
  • Algal Oil
  • Canola Oil
  • Rice Starch
  • Oat Fiber

Directions

  1. BUY SOYLENT
  2. MIX SOYLENT AT 90 DEGREE ANGLE
  3. EAT SOYLENT
